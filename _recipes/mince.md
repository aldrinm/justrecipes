ingredients:
- Onion 1 chopped
- Curry leaves
- Tomato 1 chopped
- Mince masala 1 1/2 tsp
- Salt
- Mince about 200 gms

directions:
- Saute onions till cooked
- Add curry leaves and tomato and mince masala. Fry a bit. Tomatos should become mushy
- Add mince. Fry a bit. 
- Add water if needed and cook till done
